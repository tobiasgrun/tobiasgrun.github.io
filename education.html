Statistical programming became my happy place and over time I consequently shifted my work towards data analyses, and programming became an essential part of both my work and personal projects, such as developing websites like this portfolio, and home automation systems with Raspberry Pi. Driven by my passion for innovation, I transitioned from academic research into applied software development where I combined my analytical skills with Agile methodologies and rigorous requirements engineering. My work thrives at the intersection of advanced analytical techniques and collaborative, Agile-driven development, creating innovative technical products. The combination is the sweet-spot where I excel.
