Global Nutrition and Obesity Trends

Data visualization and clustering methods are used to show global trends in nutrition and obesity, allowing to reveal global and regional public health patterns. The analysis integrates data cleaning, visualization, and clustering to interpret complex, multifaceted health data. This process translates large-scale datasets into accessible insights, guiding evidence-based decision-making in global health.
