Our Expertise

At ScyllaWorks, we specialize in designing and manufacturing advanced underwater technology tailored for the most demanding environments. Our key areas of expertise include:

Custom-Manufactured Underwater Sensors

Our high-precision sensors provide real-time environmental monitoring for research institutions, government agencies, and industrial clients. Built to withstand extreme underwater conditions, our sensors support applications in oceanography, climate studies, and marine safety.

Offshore Weather Stations

Designed to operate in harsh marine environments, our robust weather monitoring stations deliver accurate meteorological data for offshore industries, maritime operations, and environmental research.

IoT and Smart Sensor Integration

We develop cutting-edge smart sensor systems, enabling real-time data collection and seamless connectivity for continuous oceanic monitoring. Our IoT solutions empower researchers and industry professionals with instant access to critical marine data.

Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs)

Our advanced AUVs are equipped with intelligent sensor systems for deep-sea exploration, environmental surveys, and industrial applications. These highly maneuverable, autonomous systems provide unparalleled access to underwater environments.
